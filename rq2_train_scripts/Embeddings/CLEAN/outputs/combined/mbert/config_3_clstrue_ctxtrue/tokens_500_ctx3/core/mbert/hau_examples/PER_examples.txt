wani dattijo Malam Nuhu Muhammad , suka kuma
kuma sassara dansa Nasiru Nuhu a karamar Hukumar
dan uwan mamacin Muhammad Sani , sun jiyo
daga gidan Malam Nuhu , da ke
kayansu A cewar Sani dan nasa ya
suka kaiwa Malam Nuhu Hari ba .
sandan jihar Kano Abdullahi Haruna Kiyawa ya ce za
bincika hakikanin al'amarin Kiyawa mai mukamin mataimakin
sahu mai suna Sadik Hussaini da ya gudu
saan jihar Kano Abdullahi Haruna Kiyawa ne ya sanar
