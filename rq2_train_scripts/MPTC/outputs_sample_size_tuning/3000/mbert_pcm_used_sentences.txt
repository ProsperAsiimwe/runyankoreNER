Di eye wey dem put na bad tori of di goment wey no gree tackle security mata for di country .

Pipo dey cry for help for beta security of life and dere properties

Na inside all dis kasala wey we take sabi wetin di link wey dey between governance and di illegal weapon wey dey evriwia be , wey be main mata for dis time of awa national life .
As Nigeria dey go up and down for di last two decades don make plenti Nigerians sabi wetin be di poverty governance .
As e take be like dat , e go beta for us make we sabi wetin governance be for simple sense and wetin in be for inside dis context .
Di Oxford Advanced Learners Dictionary , Sixth Edition tok say governance na di way wey dem dey control one kontri or di way wey one kontri or institution deu dey control .
So , governance na di process of control and controlling activity wey dey a kontri join weapon wit im circulation .
Again , governance still fit dey look as di process wey dem goment pipo dey hold power , authority and di way wey dem dey give policy wey pertain to pesin life and economic decision for di beta beta of di pipo .
As e be , to dey run di goment go take make dem use decision wey dey good and wey go get good impact on top di management and di sharing of resources wey include moni , pesin and material .

As e be so , e get plenty reason wey explain di poverty wey never stop since for governance for Nigeria .
